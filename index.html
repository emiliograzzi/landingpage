Emilio Grazzi

Graphic and information designer, I spend my day by simplifying on paper what’s complex to say. Outcomes include wayfinding systems, maps, calendars, corporate communication, editorial and visual design.

I graduated from ISIA Urbino in 2012 (MA) after a one-year exchange at ERG Bruxelles. I’m currently freelancing, but I also collaborate with different studios and agencies for international clients. From 2017 I teach Typography and Graphic Design at Free University of Bolzano-Bozen and I enrolled the Graduate School of the Arts in Bern as PhD candidate.
