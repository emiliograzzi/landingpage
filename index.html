Emilio Grazzi

Graphic and information designer, I spend my day by simplifying on paper what’s complex to say. Outcomes include wayfinding systems, maps, diagrams, corporate communication, editorial design and interactive interfaces.

I graduated from ISIA Urbino in 2012 (MA) after a one-year exchange at ERG Bruxelles. I’m currently freelancing, but I also collaborate with different studios and agencies for international clients. In 2017, I started teaching Typography and Graphic Design at Free University of Bolzano-Bozen. The same year I enrolled the Graduate School of the Arts in Bern as PhD candidate.
